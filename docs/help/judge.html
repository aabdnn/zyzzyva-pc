Judging and Defining Words

The Judge tab can be used to determine whether a word is acceptable, and the word's definition if it is acceptable.

Judging a Word

Judging a word can be useful if you only want to know whether a word is acceptable, and don't want to know its definition. For example, in the middle of playing a word game, knowing a word's definition may change the way the word is treated by the players. A player who might not otherwise have tried to add an S to the end of a word may feel comfortable doing so after learning that the word is a noun or verb.

To Judge a word, go to the Judge tab. Type the word to be judged into the input line. Then click the Judge button. The word will be displayed, along with the word "Acceptable" or "Unacceptable".

Defining a Word

The Judge tab can be used for defining a word as well as judging it. Knowing a word's definition can be useful when studying, as it can make the word and its inflections easier to remember. To see a word's definition, type the word into the input line. Then click the Define button, or press the Enter key. The word will be displayed, along with the word "Acceptable" or "Unacceptable" and the word's definition. If the word is acceptable but has no definition available, the phrase "(no definition)" will be displayed.
